History

Image credit: TRUIC

Ms. Blanco's Sweet Life has come a long way since her humble beginning all the way back in 1999. Initially serving her sweet treats out of a vintage ice cream truck, Ms. Blanco now has a brick-and-mortar shop nestled in a quiet corner of New York. Her commitment to the finest quality locally-sourced ingredients lends her ice creams a taste that is sure to satisfy the most demanding taste buds. Over 20 years into the business, she has stayed mostly true to her original menu consisting of 5 flavors, continuing to make life sweeter with each and every scoop.

Location

You can find Ms. Blanco's Sweet Life at 13 Cornelia Street, NYC, hidden away from the hustle and bustle of the city. Her shop is a small and cozy one-story building with eat-in or takeaway options.

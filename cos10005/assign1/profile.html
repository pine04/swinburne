My profile

What I do

At present, I am focusing on my coding and language skills with the intention of becoming a proficient software developer who can work in any environment. I primarily work on front-end web development with the React.js framework, but I ocassionally work on the back-end with Express.js. As my speciality needs so much JavaScript, it is only natural that JS is my preferred programming language. As far as human language goes, I am quite proficient in English as my second language. Mis videojuegos favoritos

I used to play a lot of video games in secondary school. Some of the notable ones include Minecraft, Terraria, Stardew Valley, and League of Legends (the latter I have to quit because I developed an unhealthy obsession with it.) I guess we all eventually grow out of the things we once loved, just like they say. I have hardly any motivation to play video games anymore. However, two games still stick around with me to this day, which I ocassionally open up to play. They are Terraria and Stardew Valley. These are the only two games I would be willing to spend money on since I greatly admire the dedication the developers have put into perfecting their products.
